Get Started

An online Student\Faculty grievances management System was developed where a student|faculty tell their grievances himself

Online Student\Faculty grievances management System is Web-based software that helps you to submit the grievances induvidually online.

  • Making the registration process accessible anywhere to the student.
  • The website has been designed to be user friendly and easy to fill and hence leads to saving of time and money as compared to multiple forms filled manually by the students.
  • This portal accepts input in the of student’s and faculties personal detail, student’s registration details, faculty details etc

Development of Student\Faculty grievances system has greatly simplified previously complex tasks. Manual work is reduced to a large extent. The task of maintaining huge data can now be accomplished with a few button clicks.
